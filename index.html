Uzbek House

Discription

Well located boutique hotel right at the middle of old town. Designed in the format of old uzbek homes outside and inside, rooms are designed in modern mix of european and asian styles.

Main hall

Hall with reception and two rooms. Ceil is decorated with embroided white art Eco-friendly doors, stairs and all inner furniture.

Restaurant

Restaurant is located at the 0 floor and serves breakfasts only. It has cooler, coffee machine, sweedish table, boiler, microwave owen and all remaining stuff for comfort eating. All the meals served are from halal cuisine only. Breakfast starts at 8am and lasts 2 hours.

Second floor

Second floor consists of narrow corridor and 5 rooms. There is another cooler and paintings on the walls.

Rooms

There are 7 rooms in total: 2 twins, 3 triplets, and 2 family ones. All rooms have a bathroom inside and a window with a view of the street or garden. AC and TV are also available inside. Each room has a wardrobe with towels and slippers. Wi-Fi is equally close to all rooms and supported by the individual repeater. Some rooms have mini-fridges and tables inside, but all guests have an access to the fridge in the restaurant and table in the near reception.

Bathroom

All bathrooms are decorated with national tile and have a toilet, sink, and shower inside.

Address

Hotel is located in Samarkand city, Chorraha str #127. Right in front of the school #10
